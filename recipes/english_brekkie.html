Full English Breakfast

Above is a traditional full English breakfast that is typically served all around the world, particularly in the UK.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Bacon
  • Egg
  • Black Pudding

Step by step guide

  1. Cook sausages in pan for 15 mins
  2. Cook bacon in pan for 15 mins.
  3. Create whatever kind of egg you want.
  4. Cook black pudding in pan for 15 mins.
