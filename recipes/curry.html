Chicken with rice and peppers

Above is a curry dish derived from the mediterranean, more precisely Greece.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Black Bean Sauce
  • Mediterranean Rice
  • Bell Peppers

Step by step guide

  1. Cut up chicken and cook for 35 mins.
  2. Add sauce to chicken at 15 mins and stir until well mixed.
  3. Boil rice for 10 mins with lid on pot.
  4. Chop up peppers and add either cooked or uncooked to dish.
